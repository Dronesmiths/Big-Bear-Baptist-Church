Answers to some questions you may have

? What should I expect on my first visit?

After our 9:30 AM worship service, we have Sunday School at 10:45 AM with free refreshments and fellowship. We would love for you to join us!

? What about my children?

Loving nursery care is available on Sundays for infants and toddlers through age 2. The nursery is also available for moms to use during any service as needed, or you are welcome to keep them in the service with you.

Your children will also have the opportunity to be a part of our youth program with age-appropriate classes that help them grow spiritually and make godly friendships.

? Is there a dress code?

There is not a dress code at Big Bear Baptist Church. You will find that many of our members wear traditional “Sunday” dress, while others come more casually. Our greatest desire is for you to feel welcome and encouraged on your first visit.

? Am I expected to participate in the offering?

No. We want our services to be a gift to you. Please don’t feel any pressure or obligation to participate in the offering as a guest. We hope you find our church home to be a warm place that glorifies God through His Word.
