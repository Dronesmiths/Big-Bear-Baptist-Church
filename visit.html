Kids & Families

Loving nursery care is available on Sundays for infants and toddlers through age 2. Your children will also have the opportunity to be a part of our youth program with age appropriate classes that help them grow spiritually.

Atmosphere

You can expect a warm, friendly atmosphere with traditional and conservative hymns, special groups, and soloists. The music is followed by a practical, biblical message from Pastor Elwart.

What to Expect

  • Biblical and practical preaching
  • Warm welcome from friendly teachers and staff
  • No dress code—come as you are
  • Great music and solid Bible teaching

Community Care

Community Fellowship — Sundays at 10:45 AM

After our Sunday worship service we gather for a time of fellowship and community care. You’re welcome to join us for coffee, conversation, and prayer—or serve alongside us.
