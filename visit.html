Frequently Asked Questions

? What should I expect on my first visit?

After our 9:30 AM worship service, we have Sunday School at 10:45 AM with refreshments and fellowship. We would love for you to join us!

? What about my children?

Nursery care is available on Sundays for infants and toddlers through age 2. Families are also welcome to keep children in the service.

Age-appropriate classes are available to help children grow spiritually and build friendships.

? Is there a dress code?

We welcome you to come as you are in comfortable, casual attire. Our greatest desire is for you to feel welcome and encouraged.

? Am I expected to participate in the offering?

No. If you're visiting, please know there is no expectation for you to give. We're grateful for your presence with us.
