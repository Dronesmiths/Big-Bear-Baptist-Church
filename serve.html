Serve Together

Serve with a Willing Heart

Serving at Big Bear Baptist Church is simple. We welcome willing hands and humble hearts to help us love our neighbors, share the gospel, and encourage the body of Christ.

  • 1

    Sunday Hospitality

    Arrive a little early to welcome families, guide guests, and prepare the sanctuary for worship at 9:30 AM.

  • 2

    Outreach & Fellowship

    Help with our Sunday morning fellowship and local outreach efforts to share Christ's love in Big Bear City.

  • 3

    Prayer & Follow-Up

    Encourage people through prayer, visits, and notes through the week, especially after Thursday Bible Study at 7:00 PM.

Weekly Rhythm

Every gathering is an opportunity to serve. Here are the moments when extra hands are most helpful.

Sunday Worship

9:30 AM

10:45 AM

Bible Study

Scripture Guided

People First

Serving means noticing needs, listening well, and showing Christlike compassion.

Prayer Saturated

We pray before, during, and after we serve—asking the Lord to bear fruit in every life.

Questions

Serving Basics

If you are willing, we will walk with you step by step.

Do I need experience? ▼

No. We will show you what to do and serve alongside you until you feel comfortable.

What about serving with children? ▼

Children’s helpers complete a simple orientation and background check so families can feel at ease.

Can I serve once a month? ▼

Yes. We will work with your schedule and plug you in where you can be faithful and fruitful.

How do I start? ▼

Email pastorelwart@icloud.com or talk with us after any gathering. We will pray with you and help you begin.
