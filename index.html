Our Desire

Founded on a simple desire: to be a place where the Bible isn't just preached, but where its truth changes our real-world lives here in Big Bear City.

Our Family

We are neighbors following Jesus together. We believe in Worship & Word—longing for God's presence and grounding our lives in the Scriptures.

Our Approach

You don't need to be perfect to belong. We are ordinary people serving an extraordinary Savior, committed to caring for one another through expository preaching.

Meet Pastor Elwart

Pastor Elwart shepherds our church family with a heart for expository preaching and genuine pastoral care. He believes the Bible should shape every area of life—not just Sunday mornings.

What a Sunday actually feels like.

It's relaxed, grounded, and focused on things that matter.

Grounded Teaching

"We spend time listening to what the Bible has to say about our real lives. No hype, just honest conversations about following Jesus that make sense on a Monday morning."

Honest Worship

"We sing songs that mean something. We pray for each other because life is hard and we know we need help."

Afterwards, we stick around to talk. Most of the best moments happen over a cup of coffee in the hallway.
