A place where faith meets real life.

Big Bear Baptist Church was founded on a simple desire: to be a place where the Bible isn't just preached, but where its truth changes our real-world lives here in Big Bear City.

We are a family of neighbors following Jesus together. Whether you've walked with God for years or you're just starting to ask questions, you are welcome here. We believe in Worship & Word—longing for God's presence and grounding our lives in the Scriptures.

You don't need to be perfect to belong here. We are ordinary people serving an extraordinary Savior, committed to caring for one another through expository preaching and the public reading of Scripture.

Ordinary people, no pedestals.

Pastor Elwart is our pastor, but he’s really just part of the family. You’ll usually see him at the door holding it open for someone or chatting over coffee after the service.

What a Sunday actually feels like.

It’s relaxed, grounded, and focused on things that matter.

Grounded Teaching

"We spend time listening to what the Bible has to say about our real lives. No hype, just honest conversations about following Jesus that make sense on a Monday morning."

Honest Worship

"We sing songs that mean something. We pray for each other because life is hard and we know we need help."

Afterwards, we stick around to talk. Most of the best moments happen over a cup of coffee in the hallway.
