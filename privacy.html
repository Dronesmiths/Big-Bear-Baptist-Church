Privacy Policy

At Big Bear Baptist Church, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This policy explains how we handle your data with care and transparency.

Information We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as your name, email address, or phone number when you:

  • Fill out a "Plan Your Visit" form.
  • Sign up for our newsletter or community updates.
  • Make a donation through our giving platform.
  • Email or call us directly.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to connect with you, provide the services you've requested, and keep you informed about church life. We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. Online giving is processed through secure, industry-standard third-party providers.
