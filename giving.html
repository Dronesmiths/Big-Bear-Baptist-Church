Give Generously

Your generosity fuels the mission of Big Bear Baptist Church—reaching our community, caring for the hurting, and proclaiming the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Why We Give

Giving is an act of worship and a tangible expression of our gratitude to God for His abundant grace. When we give, we participate in the work God is doing in Big Bear City and beyond.

Worship & Obedience

Giving is a response to God's provision and an act of faith that honors Him.

Ministry & Outreach

Your gifts enable Sunday services, community care, missions, and local care.

Building God's Kingdom

Every dollar is stewarded faithfully to advance the gospel and care for people.

Community Impact

Together, we meet real needs and demonstrate Christ's love in Big Bear City.

Biblical Foundation

"Every man according as he purposeth in his heart, so let him give; not grudgingly, or of necessity: for God loveth a cheerful giver." — 2 Corinthians 9:7 (KJV)

We believe giving should be done joyfully, generously, and in proportion to how God has blessed us. Whether large or small, every gift matters.
