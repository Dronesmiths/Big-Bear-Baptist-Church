Our History

By God’s grace, Big Bear Baptist Church began as a small gathering of believers who simply wanted to worship, pray, and share the gospel in Big Bear City. Through ordinary seasons of faithfulness—meeting in homes, opening the Scriptures, serving neighbors—the Lord has led us step by step. Our story is not about a brand or a method; it is the testimony of God forming a people by His Word, for His glory, and for the good of our community.

We continue to trust Jesus to build His church as we follow Him with humility, hold fast to Scripture, and love those He places in our path.

What We Believe

The Scriptures

We believe in the plenary, verbal inspiration and authority of the Scriptures. The King James Version is our official translation.

Salvation by Grace

We believe in salvation by grace through faith, not works. Men are justified by faith alone through the merit of Jesus Christ.

The Triune God

We believe in the Triune God: God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit.

The Local Church

We believe in the local church as God's means of spreading the Gospel in this age.

What Drives Us

Our mission is not to become a brand, but to become a body—ordinary people transformed by an extraordinary Savior.

Following Jesus — His Way

Jesus did not build the church through marketing plans or corporate models. He walked with people, served quietly, and taught truth plainly. We aim to follow His pattern of sincerity and sacrifice.

Making Disciples — Not Consumers

We focus on producing disciples who walk in holiness and love truth, rather than just spectators. We measure faithfulness by obedience and repentance, not just attendance.

Led by the Spirit — Not Systems

True ministry flows from abiding in Christ. We depend on God’s Word and His Spirit for guidance, choosing biblical integrity over worldly leadership philosophies.

Loving People Over Performance

Ministry is not a performance—it is presence, compassion, and sacrifice. We walk together, opening the Scriptures and praying, until Christ be formed in us.
